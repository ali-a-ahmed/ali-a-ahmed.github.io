Overview

Power World Simulator is a high-performance power system simulation tool used for modeling and visualizing electrical grids. In this project, I used Power World to simulate fault conditions, study power flow, and evaluate grid stability under different scenarios.

What I Did

  • Created a model of a multi-bus electrical power system.
  • Simulated short circuit and line fault conditions.
  • Analyzed voltage profiles and power flow during disruptions.
  • Validated system performance under varying load demands.

Tools Used

  • Power World Simulator
  • MATLAB (for supporting calculations and graphs)

Key Learnings

This project helped me develop a strong understanding of how real-world power grids are modeled, monitored, and improved through simulation. It also deepened my skills in power systems analysis and fault-tolerant design.
